The CANOPy Sylvatic Room is a 60 m² lab at CZU's Faculty of Forestry and Wood Sciences, equipped with immersive VR, 7.1 spatial audio, climate control, and biometric monitoring.

The pilot study runs from January 2026 and compares three exposure conditions (digital forest, real forest, control room) on salivary cortisol, heart rate variability, and subjective restoration experience.
